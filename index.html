Leaders Come and Go.

But they never fail to bring about a revolution.

"You can quote them, disagree with them, glorify, or vilify them. But the only thing you can't do is ignore them. Because they change things. They push the human race forward. And while some may see them as the crazy ones, we see genius. Because the people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world, are the ones who do."

So what do I want to become in life?

A leader. A change maker.

The very existence of humanity is being challenged by countless issues such as terrorism, climate change, over-population, religious conflicts, food and water scarcity and poverty. In such times of despair, what the world needs is a global leader.
A man whom the masses can trust and lean on. Someone who does not just has opinions but also carries out actions, such a man has strength in his character. And he is what the world needs right now.

But how would I bring about that change?

Change through innovation.

If technology has brought in a lot of problems, it is equally true that it is also capable of solving a huge amount of our problems. It only depends on how someone is using it.
The 21st century is the age of technology. A lot of innovations take place all the time. Innovations are what are capable of solving problem at a large scale, even if they have small beginnings.

Do we always get what we want in life?

It is a matter of choice, not chance.

The answer to this is no. But if we want to be successful as bad as we want to breathe, then we'll be successful. The key here is to dream big and never giving up.
And if we are ever low on motivation, we can always gain inspiration from the leaders who have changed the destiny of mankind through their genius and hardwork.
